Understanding ROI and Engagement Rate

Engagement Rate: Engagement Rate is the percentage of an influencer's audience that interacts with their content. A higher engagement rate typically indicates a more active and engaged audience.Engagement Rates can reach up to 10—WOW!

  • 1%-3%: Normal
  • 3%-6%: Good
  • 6%-10%: Excellent

ROI (Return on Investment): ROI measures the profitability of a campaign relative to its cost. It is calculated as the ratio of net profit to the cost of the campaign. ROI values start from 10, which is already good, and can go up to 29, indicating exceptionally profitable campaigns.

  • 10-15: Good – The campaign is profitable, making 10 to 15 times the cost in profit. This shows the campaign is performing well.
  • 15-20: Very Good – The campaign is doing even better, making 15 to 20 times its cost in profit. This level of ROI indicates strong performance.
  • Above 20: Excellent – The campaign is highly successful, making more than 20 times its cost in profit. The higher the number, the better it is, with values up to 29 being outstanding.
Best Practices

When choosing an influencer, consider both their Engagement Rate and ROI. A higher engagement rate often leads to better audience interaction, but ROI is crucial to ensure your marketing efforts are profitable. Aim for influencers who strike a good balance between these metrics to achieve your campaign goals.
